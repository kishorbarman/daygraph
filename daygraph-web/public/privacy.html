DayGraph Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

DayGraph stores your activity logs, analytics summaries, and AI-generated insights in your Firebase project. We use this data only to provide the product features you request.

Data we store

  • Account profile (name, email, photo, timezone).
  • Activity logs and optional mood/energy ratings.
  • Derived analytics (daily/weekly stats, correlations, recaps).
  • Operational telemetry for AI quality and reliability.

How we use data

  • To show your timeline, insights, chat responses, and suggestions.
  • To compute aggregate trends and weekly recaps.
  • To monitor latency/error/cost of AI features.

Sharing

DayGraph does not sell your personal data. Data is processed by infrastructure providers needed to run the app (Firebase and AI APIs).

Your controls

  • You can edit or delete individual activity logs in the app.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • For deletion/export requests, contact support at support@daygraph.app.
